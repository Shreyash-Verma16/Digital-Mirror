🪞 Digital Mirror Prompt

You are an expert behavioral analyst with access only to our previous conversations and any memories you've been explicitly allowed to retain.

Your goal is to create the most accurate psychological profile possible based only on evidence from our conversations. Do not invent facts or make unsupported assumptions. Clearly distinguish between facts, reasonable inferences, and uncertainty.

## Part 1 — What You Know About Me

Summarize everything you've learned about me, including:
* My biggest goals
* My interests
* My recurring challenges
* My habits
* My communication style
* My values
* My strengths
* My weaknesses
* My current projects

---

## Part 2 — Pattern Analysis

Analyze the patterns behind everything you've observed.
Answer the following:
1. What are my three greatest strengths?
2. What are my three biggest weaknesses?
3. What habits keep repeating?
4. What mistakes do I make over and over?
5. What am I avoiding?
6. What fears or limiting beliefs seem to influence my decisions?
7. What motivates me the most?
8. What usually drains my energy or enthusiasm?
9. What type of work or lifestyle seems to fit me best?
10. What's one thing about me that I probably don't realize myself?

---

## Part 3 — The Future

Based on my current patterns—not guesses or supernatural prediction—answer:
* If I continue exactly as I am today, where will I most likely be in 3 years?
* What opportunities am I likely to miss?
* What risks am I should watch out for?
* What is the biggest obstacle between me and my goals?
* What single change would have the highest impact on my future?

---

## Part 4 — Brutal Honesty

Do not protect my feelings.
Tell me:
* Three uncomfortable truths I need to hear.
* One belief I should question.
* One habit I should stop immediately.
* One habit I should build immediately.
* What would disappoint my future self if nothing changed?

---

## Part 5 — Final Verdict

Give me:
* A one-paragraph psychological profile.
* A score out of 10 for:
  * Discipline
  * Consistency
  * Self-awareness
  * Decision-making
  * Growth mindset

Explain every score.

Finish with one sentence that summarizes the biggest lesson from everything you've learned about me.
